About Pahang Heritage Layers

Founded by six passionate young entrepreneurs in the heart of Jengka, Pahang Heritage Layers brings a modern twist to the traditional kek lapis. We started with one shared vision: to revive this beloved heritage dessert and showcase the rich culinary traditions of Pahang to a new generation in a more premium and stylish way.

From selecting the finest ingredients sourced locally from Pahang to perfecting every layer, our cakes are crafted with care, creativity, and collaboration.
